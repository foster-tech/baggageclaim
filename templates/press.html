PRESS

Baggage Claim Matching is a platform designed to pair people for romantic relationships. It operates under the premise that emotional and lifestyle “baggage,” is not a flaw, but rather the most important characteristic in defining long term relationship success.

The ethos of Baggage Claim is in the philosophy that vulnerability should be openly acknowledged and embraced. Baggage Claim Matching and its unique assessment-based algorithm were created by a group of innovators including: a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, a human dynamics and Psychology expert, a renowned dating coach, and an emotional intelligence researcher.

The platform launched in 2021. It was publicly introduced on the Bravo TV network and has received acclaim from practicing members of both the American Psychological Association (APA) and the California Associate of Marriage and Family Therapists (CAMFT).
