A more scientific approach to dating.

Most dating websites throw hundreds of matches aimlessly your way. If we want to date like primates, we can rely mainly on physical appearance and first impressions. However, this does not promote long-term relationship compatibility. 

Baggage Claim’s algorithm evaluates the strength of matches based on 89 different criteria. Using objective weighting methodology, it allows users to easily apply what the principles of psychological dynamics and the science of relationships have revealed. 

Created by a licensed marriage and family therapist, an expert dating coach, and a human dynamics/emotional intelligence researcher, Baggage Claim applies years of evidence-based research to maximize the success of dating.
