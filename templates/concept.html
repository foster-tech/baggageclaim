Baggage Claim is a unique dating platform that pairs users based on the compatibility of their so-called flaws – their emotional and lifestyle “baggage.” Baggage Claim’s premise is that true compatibility is more a function of complementary imperfections of than any other trait. 

People can find so many ways to get along - that’s the easy part. The difficulty is for partners to get along when they find their traits clashing. With divorce rates at an all-time high, and more options than ever – with less fulfillment than ever – it’s time to look at how people pick their partners, and why it isn’t working. One need only look around at relationships to see that when they end, it is almost always because of people’s inability to accept or cope with each other’s particular brand of baggage.

So, if baggage isn’t a flaw, what is it? Imperfections are simply individual characteristics that make up a part of who we are. These characteristics are a culmination of personality, past experience, and individual choice. We’ve been going about the role of baggage all wrong! Instead of sweeping it under the rug, baggage should be the primary factor in dating. Therefore, Baggage Claim focuses on the need for assessing baggage as the key component of compatibility. As we have become spoiled by choice, we have lost sight of an old truth. Perhaps Jane Austin said it best, “It is our imperfections that make us so perfect for one another.”

Baggage Claim Matching is unlike other dating platforms that encourage users to present a polished and idealized version of themselves. Baggage Claim was founded on the belief that baggage is not an imperfection. Rather, it only becomes an imperfection when paired with the wrong person. Therefore, baggage should not be hidden, nor prohibit people from pursuing the basic human desire to find love.

Baggage Claim Matching is a dating platform that assesses the compatibility of its users based on 89 essential elements. Its proprietary algorithm was developed by a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, human dynamics and Psychology expert, a renowned dating coach, and an emotional intelligence researcher. It employs a holistic approach to quantifying compatibility. Baggage Claim’s unique measuring tool encompasses lifestyle, emotional tendencies, behavioral patterns and personal outlook. The interface goes beyond illuminating and computing users’ baggage. It also provides research-based tips on the way each combination of individual traits would unfold in a potential relationship. The platform further prepares matches for success by simulating how interactions will most likely play out.

Baggage Claim Matching is currently available globally for web browsers. The mobile app will be available soon for iOs and android.
